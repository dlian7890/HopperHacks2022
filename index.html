Don't Smoke Be Woke

1 cigarette you smoke is 11 minutes shaved off your life

1 pack of cigarette is 1.5 months shaved off your life

1 pack a day for a year is almost 2 months saved off your life

Smoking Harms

Smoking has been proven to cause lung disease by damaging airways and the air sacs in the lungs. Lung diseases that are linked to smoking are Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease, emphysema and chronic bronchitis. Cigarette Smoking has been found to cause most cases of lung cancer and combined with asthma, can trigger an asthma attack or make it worse.

Electronic Cigarettes

Electronic Cigarettes or E-Cigs have been shown to expose the user to fewer toxicants than smoking tobacco cigarettes but the healthiest option is to not vape or smoke. E-cigarettes can contain harmful ingredients including particles that can be inhaled deep into the lungs, flavorants such as diacetyl, a chemical linked to serious lung disease.

Adolescent Exposure

E-cigarettes has been found to contain volatile organic compounds, and heavy metals such as nickel, tin, and lead. E-Cigs have been rising in popularity with a younger crowd but it has been shown that adolescent exposure to nicotine can cause addiction and harm the developing brain.
